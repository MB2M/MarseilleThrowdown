Individual Score :

The athlete's score is the time taken to complete the event. If he does not complete the wod within 20 minutes, the athlete's score is the number of repetitions completed.

Team Score :

Each athlete's score is the time taken to complete the event. If he does not complete the wod within 20 minutes, the athlete's score is 20 mins to which is added 1 second penalty for each repetition not performed. The team score is the sum of everyone's times.
